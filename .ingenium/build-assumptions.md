## Assumptions used for this draft

- Brand direction was not provided, so the draft keeps the current monochrome fight-gym feel and refines it with a bronze accent, stronger hierarchy, and a cleaner athletic type pairing.
- The current website was treated as the main source for coach bios, pricing, contact details, social links, and photography.
- A lead-generation trial/contact form was added even though `required_forms` was blank because the intake goal is qualified leads and the requested features included lead capture.
- The primary CTA for this draft is `Book a Free Trial`, with `View Timetable` as the consistent secondary CTA.
- Pricing on the Memberships page is based on the live site crawl from March 20, 2026 and should be confirmed by the owner before launch.
- The exact weekly timetable was not recreated as a day-by-day table because the live booking system loads schedule data dynamically and the intake did not include a written timetable.
